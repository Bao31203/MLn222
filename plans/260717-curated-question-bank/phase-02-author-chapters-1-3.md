## Chuan bien soan tung cau

1. Viet cau hoi tu mot muc tieu hoc tap cu the, khong hoi `noi dung nao gan voi chu de`.
2. Xac dinh dap an va trich yeu nguon truoc khi viet phuong an nhieu.
3. Viet ba phuong an nhieu trong cung pham tru, dua tren nham lan thuong gap.
4. Doc nguoc: thu tim cach chung minh moi phuong an nhieu cung dung; neu co the, sua cau.
5. Viet giai thich 1-3 cau, neu ro ly do dap an dung va diem de nham khi can.
6. Gan `topic`, `difficulty`, `kind`, trang PDF va slide bo tro.

## Nguyen tac chat luong

- Khong dung `tat ca cac dap an tren`, `ca A va B`, meo do dai hoac phuong an vo ly.
- Han che cau phu dinh; neu bat buoc, lam noi bat tu `KHONG` va dam bao chi mot ngoai le.
- Khong cat cau bang dau ba cham; khong dung trich dan thieu chu the.
- Bai tinh phai du du kien, don vi va cach giai trong phan `explanation`.
